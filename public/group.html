  • Project description:

    Our sources of information are aimed at analyzing the impact on the happiness of a country analysing different paraments as GPD, generosity, confidence, families, trust on governments and freedom of a nation. We are going to use data from 2015, 2016 and 2017. Each of will have 2 common data (country and year) and the rest will be three different data with which we will be able to compare and analysing the impact of some data over others.
